Project Title: Superstore Sales Analysis

Showcasing the journey from raw data to a final dashboard.

1. The Challenge & Raw Data

The goal of this project was to analyze Superstore sales data and uncover insights to support business decision-making. The raw dataset consisted of thousands of transaction records, including details on orders, customers, products, regions, shipping modes, and discounts. Initial challenges included missing values, inconsistent date formats, and redundant entries that needed to be standardized before analysis.

2. Data Cleaning & Preparation

The dataset was cleaned and transformed using Power Query in Power BI. Key steps included removing duplicate rows, handling null values, and ensuring consistency in categorical variables such as product categories and regions. New calculated fields were created using DAX, including profit margins, sales per customer segment, and discount impact. This preparation ensured that the dataset was reliable, accurate, and ready for analysis.

3. Analysis & Key Insights

The analysis focused on identifying sales and profit trends across regions, product categories, and customer segments. Insights revealed that while technology products generated the highest sales, office supplies delivered stronger profit margins. The data also showed that high discounts often led to reduced profitability, especially in the furniture category. Regional analysis highlighted that the West region performed the best in terms of revenue, while the South showed potential growth opportunities. These findings provided clear guidance on pricing strategy and regional focus.

4. The Final Dashboard

The final Power BI dashboard provided an interactive platform for stakeholders to explore sales and profitability performance. It included KPIs such as total sales, total profit, and average discount, along with drill-down visuals by category, sub-category, and customer segment. Regional performance maps and trend charts allowed quick identification of strengths and weaknesses. This dashboard enabled data-driven decision-making by offering a clear, real-time view of business performance from raw data to insights.
